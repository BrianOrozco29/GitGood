Super Smash Bros. Melee

A game that's withstood the test of time, Melee features characters from multiple Nintendo franchises. It is well-known for its frantic, high-speed gameplay and steep learning curve.

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

The latest game in the Smash Bros. franchise, Ultimate boasts a roster composed of every fighter in the series' history. Released in December 2018, this entry aims to makes its mark on today's eSports climate.

Dragon Ball FighterZ

Developed by Arc System Works, FighterZ takes the characters from the beloved Dragon Ball franchise and puts them in a visually stunning tag-team fighting game, whose art style is very akin to the anime. The game is praised for it's lightning fast combos and flashy special attacks.

Street Fighter V

Capcom's flagship franchise, Street Fighter V needs know introduction. This entry in the legendary fighting series is the epitome of "simple to learn, but hard to master" gameplay that keeps its fans enthralled by it's neutral-heavy nature.

Tekken 7

Often touted as one the more difficult fighting games, Bandai Namco's Tekken 7 is a staple of the fighting game community. This entry, like the others, is known for its 3D fighting mechanics, devastating punish game, and its very releastic animations.
